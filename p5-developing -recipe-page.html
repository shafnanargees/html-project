Grandma's Secret Apple Pie


This apple pie has been in my family for
generations and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sozed apples,peeled and sliced

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375 0 F (190 0 C )
  2. in a bowl mix the sugar and cinnamon
  3. Toss the apple in the sugar- cinnamon mixture
